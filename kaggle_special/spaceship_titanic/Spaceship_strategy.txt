# Spaceship Titanic - DEGF Reasoning Strategy

## Overview
This competition involved identifying which passengers were transported to another dimension. The strategy focused on 'Technical Grounding' - identifying physical constraints of the ship and group behaviors.

## Process
1. **Expenditure Logic**: People in CryoSleep cannot spend money on amenities (Spa, VRDeck, etc.). This provided a deterministic logical anchor for imputing missing CryoSleep values.
2. **Group Dynamics**: Passengers in the same 'Group' (derived from PassengerId) often share the same HomePlanet, Destination, and Cabin features.
3. **ML Model**: Used a RandomForest with deep trees to capture these reasoning paths and interactions between technical status (CryoSleep) and group context.

## Results
- The deterministic 'Expenditure' logic served as a G-grounded anchor, improving the quality of inputs to the machine learning model.
